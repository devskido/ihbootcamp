Student Test Score Management System

This page runs the score calculator script.

Enter a test score when prompted to see your grade and status!

Grading Scale:

  • S: 100+ points - Super!!
  • A: 90-99 points - Excellent work!
  • B: 80-89 points - Good job!
  • C: 70-79 points - Satisfactory performance.
  • D: 60-69 points - Needs improvement.
  • F: Below 60 points - Please try harder!

Note: 5 bonus points are automatically added to your score!

How to open console:
• Windows/Linux: Press F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I
• Mac: Press Cmd+Option+I
• Then click on the "Console" tab
